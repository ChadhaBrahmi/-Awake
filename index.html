About the project

!awake explores how language models can expand minimal human input into structured, imaginative output. You give a short dream idea, and the system tries to grow it into a full narrative. This is both a creative experiment and a learning project focused on understanding machine learning and generative models from the ground up.

How does it work?

1. You write a dream

You describe your dream in one or two sentences. It can be short, strange, or incomplete.

2. !awake interprets it

The AI reads your idea and uses a language model trained on stories to understand the mood, setting, and meaning.

3. It writes the story

The model expands your dream into a full narrative with atmosphere, details, and a proper ending.
